## 2. THE PHYSICS ENGINE (The Silk Glide)

- **Mass**: Lightweight. Feel like gliding on ice. NO heavy mass simulation.
- **Input Logic**: Interaction point (clicker) has **Zero Latency** (1:1 movement).
- **Visual Logic**: The Rocket (Gold Pen Tip) follows with a smooth lerp.
- **Features**:
    - **Golden Headlights** at the tip.
    - **Dynamic Rocket Thrusters** at the tail (flare on movement). [Source 742, 743]
- **Global Parallax**: The Main Logo (Hero) and The Robot (Uplink) must rotate subtly in opposition to cursor movement.
